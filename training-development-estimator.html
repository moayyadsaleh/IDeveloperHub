ID Training Time Estimator

I built this tool to help my fellow instructional designers plan e-learning projects more easily. This tool uses proven industry benchmarks to keep things on track.

DESCRIPTION

Need to figure out how long your instructional design tasks will take? This tool’s got you covered! if you’re whipping up slides for a self-paced course, creating videos for microlearning, sketching out storyboards for an instructor-led session, or building interactive eLearning content, planning just got a whole lot easier. This tools helps you estimate timelines for common ID tasks scriptwriting, video production, and quality assurance, etc... Add all your tasks, enter the details (like how many slides, pages, or minutes), choose the complexity, and boom! you get a training development estimating for the projects you are working on in seconds!

SEE IT IN ACTION

Curious how it works? Check out this LIVE DEMO to see the estimator in action. It’s a quick way to experience how this tool can help you plan and manage your instructional design projects more efficiently.

HOW TO USE

  1. Access the Estimator Tool: Click the link to go directly to the app: Launch the Estimator.
  2. Add Your Tasks: - Select the task type (e.g., content analysis, slide development, video production). - Enter the number of units (slides, pages, or minutes). - Choose the complexity level (Simple, Moderate, or Complex).
  3. Calculate the Total Time: - Click the "Calculate Total Time" button to see the estimated hours for your project.
  4. Add Contingency Time (Optional): - Select a contingency percentage to account for unexpected delays.
  5. Export Your Plan: - Download your project plan as a PDF. - View a clear visual pie chart breakdown of your tasks.
  6. Bookmark the Link for Future Use: - To use the estimator again, keep a copy of the link or bookmark it in your browser: https://moayyadsaleh.github.io/ID-Project-Time-Estimator/ .
