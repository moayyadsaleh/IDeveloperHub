Welcome to IDeveloperHub

Welcome to IDeveloperHub! As a fellow instructional designer, I know how frustrating it can be to work around the limitations of eLearning tools. That’s why I created this platform—to give you FREE custom code snippets that you can copy and paste right into tools like Articulate Storyline, RISE, or Captivate. These snippets are here to save you time, simplify your workflow, and add new features—whether it’s automating tasks like creating PDF certificates or adding dynamic progress bars. Just copy, paste, and let the magic happen. If you find value in my work, share it with others or consider supporting me on Patreon to keep the ideas flowing.

About the Platform
