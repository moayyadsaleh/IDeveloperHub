We all know instructional design isn’t a one-person show. That’s where IDtipsHub comes in. It's a space where you can pitch in with anything that makes life as an instructional designer a little easier — thoughts, tips, tricks, tool recommendations, or lessons you’ve learned. Ready to share what you know? Click the “+” button and throw in a tip. It’s automatically saved and styled, so no fuss. Curious what other IDs are saying? Just check out ideas by hitting “Show Tips”. Let’s make this a place where we help out each other and build up a collection of ideas that actually work in the real world. Check it out and pitch in — the community’s better when we all jump in!
