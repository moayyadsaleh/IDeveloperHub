This tracker helps learners take control of their progress by providing satisfying visuals, fun sounds, and celebratory confetti to mark their achievements. Designed to be easily embedded in e-learning courses, it allows learners to manually log their progress after each module, task, or milestone. Perfect for self-paced learning, and helps keep learners motivated and excited.

SLEEK COUNTDOWN TIMER

This modern, sleek phone-like timer is perfect for managing learning activities and time-boxing tasks. It features a transparent background that blends seamlessly with any project design. You can easily set the time, pause, stop, and reset the timer. When the countdown finishes, a sound alert notifies you that time is up. Ideal for quizzes, timed activities, and task pacing.
