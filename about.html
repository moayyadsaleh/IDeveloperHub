ABOUT IDEVELOPERHUB

IDeveloperHub is a free platform—and it will always be free. Here, you’ll find custom code snippets designed to simplify complex tasks, enhance engagement, and add new functionality to your eLearning projects. From automating repetitive processes to introducing innovative features, these tools save you time and let you focus on what matters most—creating impactful learning experiences.

This platform is always growing, with new code snippets and resources added regularly. My goal is to support instructional designers by providing tools that don’t just make your work easier—but also more enjoyable.

IDeveloperHub is growing and is free and community-driven platform. Your support will make a huge difference by sharing the platform with your network, offering feedback, or backing me on Patreon, every contribution makes a difference.
